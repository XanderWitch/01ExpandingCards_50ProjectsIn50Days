01 - Expanding Cards
Part of 50 Projects in 50 Days Udemy Course by Brad Traversy and Florin Pop

With customization by Janell Robisch of XanderWitch Design and Development

In this project, five images are set side by side using flex. They are styled and then adjusted to expand when clicked. Titles (headings) appear on the bottom of each image only when it is expanded.
